MAGGI

Maggi Jollove Wisdom Campaign

"Are You Fluent in Jollof?" is an interactive digital campaign for MAGGI designed to celebrate the cultural significance of Jollof rice through the "Jollove Wisdom" concept, challenging the community to prove their "fluency" through creative quotes.

Problem

MAGGI needed a fresh, interactive way to engage their community and celebrate Jollof culture while driving high volumes of social media participation and brand affinity.

Solution

I developed a high-energy 30-second animation centered on the "Jollove Wisdom" concept, using bold typography and vibrant food visuals to invite audience contributions.

Value Delivered

A significant boost in brand engagement and community storytelling, with winners awarded 5,000 Naira, custom merch, and a featured spot on the official MAGGI page.

Engagement Strategy

Jollove Wisdom & Community

The campaign invites fans to create original quotes about Jollof and share them in the comments, drawing inspiration from MAGGI’s established "Jollove Wisdom" series.

To incentivize participation, the five most creative entries were selected to win 5,000 Naira and a customized MAGGI shirt, with the winning quotes professionally showcased on the official MAGGI page.
