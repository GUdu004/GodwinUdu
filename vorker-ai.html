Vorker AI: Motion Design, Animation and Video Edit

A high-energy, founder-led launch video for Henrik and Vorker.ai. Vorker is an AI tool designed to help small businesses execute smarter by connecting directly to their workflows, moving beyond mere idea generation to actual completion.

Problem

Small businesses are overwhelmed by too many tools that don't talk to each other. They need a way to actually get work done, not just generate more ideas in a vacuum.

Solution

A "scroll-stopping" launch moment featuring personal, dynamic storytelling intercut with clean platform visuals and high-energy kinetic typography to drive curiosity and clicks.

Value Delivered

A high-impact motion asset that highlights Vorker's core value proposition: execution over ideation. Fast-paced editing and burned-in captions ensure engagement across all social channels.

Dynamic Delivery

Kinetic Typography

To maximize engagement, especially on muted social feeds, we implemented high-energy kinetic typography. Text is synced to speech, highlighting key phrases like "work smarter" and "less busywork."

The style is punchy and moves with the pace of the edit—using pops, slides, and snaps to keep visual momentum without overwhelming the viewer with full sentences.

Momentum & Flow

Modern Startup Aesthetic

The edit favors quick cuts (every 1-2 seconds) to maintain energy. Talking head footage is intercut with platform recordings to focus on actions, flows, and outcomes rather than static demos.

This approach creates a "north star" of curiosity, leading the viewer through a narrative journey from tool overwhelm to streamlined execution.
