## Project: Maggi Jollove Wisdom

**Role:** Senior Motion Designer
**Client:** Maggi

## Duration: 30 Seconds Animation

### **Project Overview**

"Are You Fluent in Jollof?" is an interactive digital campaign for MAGGI designed to celebrate the cultural significance of Jollof rice. The project centers on the "Jollove Wisdom" concept, challenging the community to prove their "fluency" in Jollof culture through creative quotes. As the Senior Motion Designer, I translated this call-to-action into a 30-second animation designed to drive audience participation and brand engagement.

### **Campaign Strategy & Incentive**

The campaign invites fans to create original quotes about Jollof and share them in the comments, drawing inspiration from MAGGI’s established "Jollove Wisdom" series. To incentivize participation, the five most creative entries are selected to win:

- **Prizes:** 5,000 Naira and a customized MAGGI shirt.
- **Digital Feature:** The winning quotes are professionally showcased on the official MAGGI page, giving contributors high-profile recognition within the community.

### **Creative Execution**

The animation focuses on bold typography and vibrant MAGGI branding to highlight the "Jollove Wisdom" theme. By combining high-energy motion with cultural storytelling, the campaign successfully fostered a sense of community and celebrated the unique bond between the brand and its consumers.
