## Project: Nescafe Red Shopper Promotion - One push to Start Strong

**Role:** Senior Motion Designer
**Client:** Nescafe

### **Project Overview**

The Nescafe Red Shopper Promotion is a unique campaign designed to empower entrepreneurs. Unlike standard promotions, this initiative encourages consumers to purchase a NESCAFÉ 3-in-1 promo pack for a chance to win essential equipment, seed funding, and professional courses to kickstart their "new hustle." As the Senior Motion Designer, my role was to create engaging visual assets that communicate this "Start Strong" message across various digital platforms.
