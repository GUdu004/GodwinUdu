The video highlights a promotional campaign by Union Bank with the following key details:

Occasion: Union Bank is celebrating its 105th anniversary.
Grand Prize: The bank is giving away a total of 55 million naira as part of the celebration.
Participation Requirement: To be eligible, customers must save at least 10,000 naira monthly.
Eligibility: The offer is available to both new and existing customers.

## Project: Union Bank 105th Anniversary "Save & Win" Campaign

**Role:** Senior Motion Designer
**Client:** Union Bank

### **Project Overview**

To commemorate Union Bank’s 105th anniversary, I developed a high-energy motion graphics piece designed to drive customer engagement and savings deposits. The project focused on communicating a high-value incentive—a 55 million naira giveaway—while maintaining the brand's commitment to "simpler, smarter" banking.

### **Key Deliverables & Creative Execution**

- **Visual Strategy**: Utilized bold typography and dynamic transitions to highlight the core anniversary message and the substantial 55 million naira prize pool.
- **Call-to-Action Integration**: Integrated clear, instructional motion elements to explain the eligibility criteria: saving 10,000 naira or more on a monthly basis.
- **Brand Alignment**: Ensured the visual language resonated with both new and existing customers, adhering to the bank's long-standing brand identity as it reached its 105-year milestone.

### **Technical Breakdown**

- **Promotional Mechanics**: The motion design emphasizes the simplicity of the entry process, visually guiding the viewer through the requirements of the monthly 10,000 naira saving goal.
- **Messaging Architecture**:
  - **The Hook**: Opening with the 105th-anniversary celebration to establish trust and scale.
  - **The Incentive**: Highlighting the 55 million naira giveaway as the primary visual focus.
  - **The Target**: Explicitly addressing both new and existing customer segments.
- **Brand Conclusion**: Ending with the "Union Bank, your simpler, smarter" brand promise to reinforce reliability.
