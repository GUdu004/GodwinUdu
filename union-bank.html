Union Bank

Union Bank 105th Anniversary "Save & Win" Campaign

To commemorate Union Bank’s 105th anniversary, I developed a high-energy motion graphics piece designed to drive customer engagement and savings deposits. The project focused on communicating a high-value incentive—a 55 million naira giveaway—while maintaining the brand's commitment to "simpler, smarter" banking.

Problem

Union Bank needed to celebrate their 105-year milestone while driving deposits through a massive 55 million naira promotional giveaway that required clear, engaging communication of eligibility rules.

Solution

I crafted a visual strategy utilizing bold typography and dynamic transitions to highlight the prize pool, integrating instructional motion elements to explain the 10,000 naira monthly saving requirement.

Value Delivered

A high-impact campaign that successfully reached new and existing segments, reinforcing Union Bank's brand promise of "simpler, smarter" banking through reliable and scalable visual communication.

Visual Strategy

Bold Typography & Dynamic Transitions

The motion design emphasizes the simplicity of the entry process, visually guiding the viewer through the requirements of the monthly 10,000 naira saving goal.

Utilizing bold typography ensures that key messages—the 105th anniversary and the 55 million naira prize—remain the primary focus, even in fast-paced social media environments.

Messaging Architecture

The Hook, Incentive & Target

We structured the narrative to first establish trust by opening with the anniversary milestone, followed by the incentive of the giveaway, and finally addressing both new and existing customer segments.

The piece concludes with the "Union Bank, your simpler, smarter" brand promise, reinforcing the bank's long-standing reliability as it reaches its 105-year milestone.
