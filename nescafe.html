Nescafe

Nescafe Red Shopper Promotion - One push to Start Strong

A unique campaign designed to empower entrepreneurs by offering equipment, seed funding, and professional courses to kickstart their new hustle through the purchase of NESCAFÉ 3-in-1 promo packs.

Problem

Standard promotions often lack long-term value. Nescafe wanted a campaign that didn't just give away prizes but actively helped consumers build a sustainable future.

Solution

The "Red Shopper Promotion" incentivized purchases by providing the tools for a "new hustle"—including equipment and seed funding—communicated through high-energy motion graphics.

Value Delivered

Engaging visual assets across desktop and mobile platforms that effectively communicated the "Start Strong" message, driving both brand engagement and promo participation.
