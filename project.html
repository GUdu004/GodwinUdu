Amazon

Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment Plugin & Apps Website

We developed the official Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment Webflow App and WooCommerce Plugin, in partnership with Amazon. With new versions of these fulfillment extensions ready to go to market, we were tasked with designing and developing a new marketing website.

Problem

As our Amazon MCF product line expanded, customers needed an easy way to explore what we had to offer. With no dedicated website, that experience didn’t exist, and we needed to create one.

Solution

Partnering with Amazon and Webflow, we designed and developed a scalable website that showcases the full product lineup, while allowing room to grow as new plugins and apps are added.

Value Delivered

A custom Webflow website that educates users about Amazon MCF and its business benefits, while presenting the official products in a clear, professional, and engaging way.

Initial Ideation

UI/UX Design

Because the site’s focus is on education and plugins, the product itself isn’t inherently visually exciting. While we incorporated references to the plugin UI, the primary goal was to distill content so users could understand the benefits.

To achieve this, we carefully mapped Content Plans and User Journeys before translating them into a UI/UX design, which was approved by both partners before development began.

Above & Beyond

Custom Development

To create a memorable, engaging, and brand-consistent experience, we custom-coded interactions and functionality beyond native capabilities.

From the glass-effect containers and dynamic on-hover button lighting to the sticky comparison table where items gracefully fade as headings scroll over them, each detail contributes to a premium user experience.
